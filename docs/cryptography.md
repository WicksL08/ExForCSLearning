# Secret Mission: Encrypt & Decrypt

Welcome, Agent! Your mission is to decode secret messages intercepted from HQ. Each message is encrypted differently. Solve the puzzles to reveal the flags and complete your mission.

You will face **three levels**:

1. [Easy – Caesar Cipher](easy_caesar.txt)
2. [Medium – Substitution Cipher](medium_substitution.txt)
3. [Hard – Base64 + Caesar Cipher](hard_base64.txt)

Click the links above to download the challenge files and follow the instructions in each `.txt` file.

Good luck, Agent! Solve all messages and submit the flags on CTFd to complete your mission.
